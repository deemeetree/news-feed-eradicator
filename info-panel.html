About

Facebook locks you into a filter bubble. In order to better serve ads it shows you the info that gets the most likes from your friends, making it harder for you to discover new, unexpected stuff.
News Feed, in this case, becomes the main source of distraction and time wasting.
In order to avoid this, we simply block your newsfeed with links to the scientific research on Facebook (so you become more aware about how it works) as well as some practical recommendations on what you can do to avoid being locked into Facebook's filter bubble. We also left and remastered some original quotes from Facebook newsfeed eradicator app.
If you have any ideas for quotes, please submit them to the Nodus Labs

Polysingular News Feed Tuning is open source. It is based on JordWest's News Feed Eradicator and the modified source code is available on github. The scientifict research is taken from Nodus Labs, some quotes are from Polysingularity.
